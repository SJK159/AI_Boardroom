# Standard Seller Agreement — Olist Inc. Marketplace

This is the standard agreement template applied to all third-party sellers registered on the
Olist Inc. marketplace platform. Individual sellers do not negotiate bespoke terms; all sellers
operate under this single template unless a written addendum states otherwise (no addenda are
on file as of this document's last review).

## Scope
This Agreement governs the relationship between Olist Inc. ("Platform") and any third-party
seller ("Seller") registered to list and sell products through the Platform's marketplace.

## Payment Terms
Olist Inc. remits seller proceeds, net of platform commission, on a rolling basis within 14
calendar days of order delivery confirmation. Commission rate is 12% of item price, excluding
freight charges collected on the Seller's behalf.

## Service Level Agreement (SLA)
Seller shall maintain an on-time delivery rate, measured against the Platform's estimated
delivery date, of no less than 85% across any rolling evaluation period. Failure to maintain
this threshold for two consecutive review periods may result in listing suspension pending
review by the Platform's Operations team.

## Term and Renewal
This Agreement is effective as of the Seller's onboarding date and automatically renews on an
annual basis unless terminated by either party with 30 days' written notice prior to the
renewal date.

## Termination
Olist Inc. may terminate this Agreement immediately for breach of the Service Level Agreement,
fraudulent activity, or violation of the Platform's Code of Conduct. Seller may terminate with
30 days' written notice.

## Liability
Olist Inc.'s liability under this Agreement is limited to the total commission earned from the
Seller in the preceding 12 months. Neither party is liable for indirect or consequential
damages arising from this Agreement.
