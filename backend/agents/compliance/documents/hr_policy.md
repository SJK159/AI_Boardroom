# HR Policy Document — Olist Inc.

## Employment Classifications
Employees are classified as Full-Time, Part-Time, or Independent Contractor. Full-Time status
applies to employees scheduled for 32 or more hours per week. Classification determines
eligibility for benefits described elsewhere in this policy.

## Leave Policy
Full-time employees accrue 20 days of paid annual leave per calendar year, plus 10 days of paid
sick leave. Parental leave is provided at 16 weeks paid for the primary caregiver and 6 weeks
paid for the secondary caregiver, in line with company-wide benefits guidelines.

## Code of Conduct
All employees are expected to act with integrity, avoid conflicts of interest, protect
confidential company and customer information, and comply with all applicable laws and
regulations. Violations may be reported to a direct manager or the People team.

## Compensation & Performance Reviews
Base compensation is reviewed annually each January. Performance reviews occur bi-annually, in
June and December, and inform compensation adjustments and promotion decisions.

## Termination Policy
Employment may be terminated by either party with 30 days' written notice. The company reserves
the right to terminate immediately, without notice, in cases of gross misconduct, as defined by
the Code of Conduct section above.
